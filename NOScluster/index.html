This is the condensed version of the rules

  • 1. PvP

  • 2. Admin makes the decisions here.

  • 3. Don't make the admin mad. Play against other players, never against the admin.

  • 4. No PvP at admin sanctuaries, no leaving dinos or characters there, either.

  • 5. 1 hour capture times.

  • 6. Follow rules like official: Do not use cheats/hacks/exploits. Ark Code of Conduct does apply here.

  • 7. Keep it streamer friendly or get banned. Refrain from leaning on anything personal with other players anywhere on discord or in global, local, tribe, or ally chats or you will be considered for an immediate ban. Twitch ToS must be respected. No racial slurs, nothing too sexual, no doxxing, etc...

  • 8. Just have fun and rebuild if someone raids you. Clean up your fobs as soon as you are done attacking, and if its a war you can sleep or go to work and leave the fob during that time but that's it.

  • 9. No building in artifact caves. Teleporters are the only exception to the rule.

  • Big Rad and Teeni are the only admins. Please be our eyes and ears out there for rules violations and send clear evidence if you can get it.
